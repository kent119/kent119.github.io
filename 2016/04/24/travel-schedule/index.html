Travelling Schedule

发表于 2016-04-24

Day 1 - 04 Jun 2016

Flight arrived at Zurich at 8:50 04 Jun 2016.

Directly head to Hotel:

  1. Take train to Kriens
  2. Take telpher to Hotel Belleveu Kriens on the Pilatus
  3. Sleep and walk around on the mountain
  4. Have a good meal.

Day 2 - 05 Jun 2016

Things to do:

  1. Leave the hotel in the afternoon and travel along the Goldern Round Trip
  2. Go to Lucerne, and the lake.
  3. Check-in in the hotel in Alpnac

Day 3 - 06 Jun 2016

Things to do:

  1. If get up early, walk around in the small town.
  2. Leave the hotel and go to Grindelwald by train/bus
  3. Stop at Lungern for 1h
  4. Next, theres is two plans here:
    A. Stop at Brienz for 1h, and stop at Interlaken Ost for 1+h, then finally go to Grindelwald
    B. Do not stop at Brienz but can spend more time in Interlaken, before going to Grindelwald
  5. Check-in in ASPEN alpin lifestyle hotel in Grindelwald

Day 4 - 07 Jun 2016

07 and 08 Jun are Hiking days.

There are two available options: one is Faulhorn, the other is Jungfraujoch.

It seems that the hiking path to Faulhorn could be a little bit difficult than the Jungfraujoch. So we should probably climb the Faulhorn on 07 Jun and the Jungfraujoch on 08 Jun.

Path to Faulhorn:

  1. Take gondola lift to the First station
  2. There are three plans:
    A. Go to Bachalpsee lake and then turn back which may take about 2-3h
    B. Do not turn back, and spend 3 more h to Faulhorn and Bussalp, and take bus back to the town
    C. Go from First->Faulhorn->Schynige Platt, which may spend about 6 h. The view should be better but it may not easy to back to the hotel

Day 5 - 08 Jun 2016

Path to Jungfraujoch:

  1. Take train to Kleind Scheidegg, about 0.5 h.
  2. Change train to the Jungfraujoch.
  3. After getting back from the Jungfraujoch, walk along the railway from Klein to Vengen, which may take about 2h.
  4. Spend some time in Vengen then go back to the hotel.

Day 6 - 09 Jun 2016

  1. Check-out and take train to Zermatt.
  2. Take a day trip to Matthorn. First, take train to Roternbodern station and go to Riffelsee
  3. Maybe climb up to Gornergrat, if not, then take train to there
  4. Two options here:
    4.1. Take gondola to Schwarzsee lake for hiking
    4.2. Or take gondola to viist Matterhorn glacier paradise and maybe skiing
  5. Spend a night in Zermatt

Day 7 - 10 Jun 2016

  1. Leave Zermatte and check-in in Sorell Hotel Rutli, Zurich
  2. Have a day trip inside Zurich, visit museum and church.
  3. No need to be too tired, because tomorrow will leave Switzerland and have 1 stop in Paris, France.

Day 8 - 11 Jun 2016

Check-out and take plane to Paris and stop serveral hours.

If possible, go to Champs Elysees and take a picture with Arc de Triomphe, and with Eiffel tower at a long long distance.

Finnally, fly back to Hong Kong at night.
